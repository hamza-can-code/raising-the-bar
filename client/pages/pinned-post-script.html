Your Pinned Post

This post does the selling for you, no need to overproduce.

Time required

10 minutes

Frequency

Once

Where it lives

Pinned post

What This Post Does

It warms new visitors so your bio link gets clicks.

  • Explains your structure in under 30 seconds.
  • Makes new visitors understand what they’ll follow.
  • Routes attention to your bio link without selling.

Rule

This post must feel like normal content.

Keep it about your structure—skip features or “app” talk.

How to Film It

This is not a performance. It’s a simple explanation.

Do this:

  • Talk like you normally talk.
  • One take is fine.
  • Keep it 20–40 seconds.
  • End with “link in bio”.
If it feels boring, you’re doing it right.

Avoid doing this:

  • Explain features or how the product works.
  • Say “AI”, “app”, “macros”, “generator”, “software”.
  • Over-sell or sound like an advert.
  • Re-film every week. Film once and move on.

The Script

Copy this word-for-word, then deliver it in your own voice.

Hook Say this first. 
If you’ve ever wondered how I train, eat, and stay consistent — this is exactly how I do it.
Body Keep it calm and normal. 
I get asked a lot how I structure my training and meals without overthinking everything.

This is the exact system I follow myself. Nothing random. Everything has structure.

It keeps me consistent, even on busy weeks, because I don’t need to decide what to do — I just follow it.
CTA End with this. 
If you want to follow the same structure I use, it’s linked in my bio.

Quick Reminder

You are not trying to convince anyone. You are just making it easy for the right people to click.
