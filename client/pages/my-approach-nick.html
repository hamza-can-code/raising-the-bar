My Approach

Simple habits. Consistent effort. Long-term results.

This is how I approach training, nutrition, and discipline so I can keep progressing year-round. These are the principles I follow to build and maintain my physique.

Training Principles
  • Progressive overload first: add reps, control, or load over time.
  • Master the basics before chasing complexity.
  • Train with intent: quality reps and consistent execution.
Nutrition Principles
  • Build each meal around protein and whole foods.
  • Keep nutrition simple enough to stay consistent every week.
  • Use structure, not restriction, to stay lean and energized.
Discipline & Consistency
  • Show up even when motivation dips — momentum compounds.
  • Focus on repeatable daily actions over short-term extremes.
  • Track what matters and adjust quickly when needed.
Recovery & Lifestyle
  • Prioritize sleep and hydration to support performance.
  • Manage stress so training and nutrition stay sustainable.
  • Aim for progress you can maintain, not perfection for a week.

Stay patient, stay consistent, and let the basics do the heavy lifting.
