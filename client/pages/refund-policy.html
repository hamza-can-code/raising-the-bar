2. Money-Back Guarantee (where advertised)

A Money-Back Guarantee applies only when expressly stated at checkout. To qualify for a full refund, all of the following requirements must be met:

“Transaction date” refers to the date/time shown on your receipt or payment statement (UTC).

  • A written request must be emailed to info@raisingthebarapp.com within 45 calendar days of the transaction date.
  • You have used the platform for a minimum of 30 consecutive days and can provide verifiable proof of meaningful engagement, including all of:
    • ≥ 20 unique workouts completed with logged sets, reps, and weights;
    • ≥ 50 meals logged or swapped in the nutrition tracker;
    • An unbroken activity streak of ≥ 21 days visible in your account history;
    • Upon request, screen recordings and/or screenshots demonstrating live interaction with program features (we may corroborate with IP, device, or server analytics).
  • The request includes a clear, honest reason for dissatisfaction. Fabricated, back-dated, or otherwise fraudulent evidence immediately voids eligibility.
  • If you encounter any access or functionality issues, you must report them to us during the guarantee period and provide basic details (e.g., screenshots or screen recordings). We will investigate using our logs and telemetry. If our logs confirm normal service and no reproducible fault is found, such issues will not qualify for a refund.
  • Only one Money-Back Guarantee claim is permitted per customer (household/device) within any 12-month period.
  • Your account must be in good standing with no overdue or unpaid balances at the time your refund is assessed.

Refund determinations are made at our sole discretion and communicated within 14 business days of receiving a complete request. Our decision is final. This does not affect your statutory rights.

Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method only. Processing times vary by provider, but we aim to issue approved refunds within 5–10 business days of approval.

3. Subscription Billing & Cancellations

Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled. By subscribing, you agree to the following billing and cancellation terms:

  • Subscriptions are billed in advance on a recurring basis (weekly or monthly, depending on the plan selected).
  • All cancellations must be requested by contacting our support team via email at info@raisingthebarapp.com. There are currently no in-app or self-service cancellation options.
  • Cancellations schedule your subscription to end at the conclusion of your current billing cycle. You will retain full access to all premium features until that date, and no further charges will occur after the cycle ends.
  • Cancellation requests submitted after a renewal charge has been processed will apply to the next billing cycle. No pro-rated or partial refunds are provided for remaining days or weeks within an active billing period.
  • Charges already processed are final and non-refundable except as required by law or under the Money-Back Guarantee (Section 2).

If you are a consumer residing in the UK or EU, you may have the right under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 / Directive 2011/83/EU to withdraw from a digital-content contract within 14 days of purchase unless you have begun downloading, streaming, or otherwise accessing the paid features. To exercise this right (before accessing content), email info@raisingthebarapp.com with your order details and a clear statement of withdrawal.
