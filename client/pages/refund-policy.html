Refund Policy

Effective Date: 19/01/2025

1. Money-Back Guarantee Conditions

If a "Money-Back Guarantee" option was explicitly presented to you during checkout, you may qualify for a refund provided that all the following conditions are met:

  • You must request the refund by contacting us via email at support@raisingthebarcoaching.com within 30 days of your purchase date.
  • You must have followed the program as outlined in your PDF for at least 30 consecutive days.
  • Your request must include supporting evidence demonstrating compliance, such as:
    • Screenshots or photos of all of your logged workouts, including dates and exercises performed.
    • Daily calorie intake records, supported by images of meals consumed, and clearly showing the calorie information.
    • Video recordings of workout sessions to confirm adherence to the plan.
    • Other proof of adherence to the provided plan (e.g., tracking sheets or progress notes).
  • Failure to provide complete evidence as outlined above may result in your refund request being declined.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and you will be notified of the outcome within 14 business days.

Note: The Money-Back Guarantee does not apply to the following cases:

  • Personal preferences (e.g., you don’t like the program, or it didn’t meet your expectations).
  • Failure to follow the program as instructed.

2. General Refund Policy

If you do not meet the conditions set out above, fees paid for the program are non-refundable and non-exchangeable, unless otherwise required by applicable law.

3. EU Residents

As per EU regulations, you are entitled to withdraw from this contract within 14 days of purchase without giving any reason, provided that:

  • You have not downloaded or accessed the PDF program.
  • If you access or download the program, the right to withdraw is forfeited.

To exercise this right, email support@raisingthebarcoaching.com within 14 days of purchase with proof of purchase and a clear statement of your intention to withdraw.

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please send an email to support@raisingthebarcoaching.com with the following information:

  • Full name and email used during purchase.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt or confirmation email).
  • Evidence of adherence to the program (if applicable).
  • Reason for requesting the refund.

We reserve the right to request additional documentation or clarification before processing your request.
