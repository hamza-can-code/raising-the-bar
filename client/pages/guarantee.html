What this covers

This guarantee covers the difference between your total earnings and your £49.99 setup fee, calculated at day 90.

  • We use Stripe data from your creator partnership setup
  • We review eligibility against the requirements below
  • If eligible, we refund the difference

What this does not cover

This doesn’t apply if someone pays the setup fee, posts inconsistently (or not at all), or ignores the launch guidance.

  • No refund for inactivity or missing key steps
  • No refund if the correct link / CTA isn’t used
  • No refund if pinned post + bio aren’t implemented and kept live

Requirements

How you qualify

These rules are here to keep the guarantee fair. If you follow them, you’re covered.

Approved bio

You must use a bio we approve that clearly directs viewers to your app link.

Correct link

You must use the exact link we provide, and keep it live for the full 90 days.

Approved pinned post

You must publish a pinned post we approve and keep it pinned during the 90 days.

Consistent uploads

You must upload at least 3 times per week on your main platform.

Use the CTA

Your content must include a clear CTA to the app (not “DM me to buy” as the main route).

Follow launch guidance

You must follow the key setup + launch steps we provide (scripts, structure, link placement, positioning).

If you’re unsure whether you qualify at any point, message us and we’ll confirm.
