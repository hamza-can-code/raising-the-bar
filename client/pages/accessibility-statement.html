Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: 19/01/2025

1. Our Commitment

Raising The Bar Coaching is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continuously improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

2. Measures to Support Accessibility

We take the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Incorporating accessibility into our development process.
  • Conducting regular accessibility audits.
  • Providing ongoing staff training on accessibility standards.

3. Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us at:
support@raisingthebarcoaching.com

4. Technical Specifications

The accessibility of our website relies on the following technologies to function with your web browser:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

5. Limitations

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility, some limitations may exist. Below is a description of potential limitations:

  • Uploaded third-party content may not always meet accessibility standards.
  • Older browsers or assistive technologies may experience reduced compatibility.

Please let us know if you encounter any issues, so we can address them promptly.

6. Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with the latest versions of the following browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

7. Updates

We review and update this Accessibility Statement regularly to reflect improvements in our website's accessibility.
