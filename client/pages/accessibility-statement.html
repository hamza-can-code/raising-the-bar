Accessibility Statement

Effective Date: 17/05/2025

1. Our Commitment

Raising The Bar App Ltd is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to continually improve user experience and conform to current accessibility standards wherever possible.

2. Measures to Support Accessibility

To support accessibility across our platform, we take the following actions:

  • Integrate accessibility into our design and development workflows.
  • Conduct periodic audits and manual checks for compliance.
  • Educate and train our team on best practices in accessibility and inclusive design.

3. Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our platform. If you experience any barriers or have suggestions, please reach out to:
info@raisingthebarapp.com

4. Technical Specifications

Our website relies on the following technologies to function correctly and ensure accessibility:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

5. Known Limitations

While we do our best to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations. These include:

  • Occasional third-party content (e.g., embedded videos) may not meet full accessibility standards.
  • Some features may have reduced performance on older browsers or unsupported assistive technologies.

We are actively working to resolve these issues. Please report any specific problems so we can address them.

6. Browser Compatibility

Our website is optimized for the latest versions of:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

7. Ongoing Improvements

We review and update this Accessibility Statement regularly and strive to enhance accessibility across all areas of our site.
