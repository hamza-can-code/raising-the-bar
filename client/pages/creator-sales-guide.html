Creator Playbook

IKEA-style instructions for monetising your audience

How to Drive Sales to Your Branded App

Follow this exactly. Do not improvise.

Section 0: Read This First

Remove thinking before you even scroll.

  • This page replaces all marketing advice.
  • You are not required to be creative.
  • You are not selling software.
  • Your job is only to route attention.

Rule

If you follow these steps, the system works.

If you skip or change steps, results drop.

Section 1: The Only Traffic Flow

This is the only path you optimise.

Content
→
Bio Link
→
Website
→
Sale
  • You post content (unchanged).
  • Attention routes to your bio.
  • The website handles conversion.

You do not sell in captions.

You do not DM sell.

You do not explain features.

Step 1

Your Link Lives Here (and nowhere else)

Time required: 1 minute.

Do this:

  • One link in bio.
  • Same link everywhere.

Do NOT do this:

  • Multiple links.
  • Link trees.
  • Feature descriptions.
When someone opens your profile, they should immediately know where to click.

Step 2

Your Bio Is a Call to Action

Time required: 1 minute.

Your bio must make people curious enough to click, not informed enough to understand.

Good bio patterns (pick one):

  • “This is the system I follow to stay consistent ↓”
  • “My full training + nutrition structure ↓”
  • “How I train and eat year-round ↓”

Bad bio patterns:

  • AI
  • Features
  • Credentials
  • “Online coach”
If your bio explains instead of invites, it’s wrong.

Step 3

Your Pinned Post Does the Selling

Create one pinned post (mandatory).

1 pinned post minimum. Everything else stays normal.

This post must answer: “How do you train and stay consistent?”

Relatable hook
Authority (this is what I follow)
CTA (link in bio)

Copy the pinned post script. Post it. Pin it.

Use the pinned post script
New visitors should understand your system before scrolling.

If you skip this step, the rest of the system underperforms.

Stop Here — Quick Check

Do this before posting anything else.

  • ☑ Bio link updated (one link only)
  • ☑ Bio text changed to a CTA
  • ☑ At least one pinned post live

If all three are not done, stop here.

Step 4

Keep Posting Normally (Do NOT Change This)

This is where people mess up. Stay consistent.

90% of posts → Desire

  • Training
  • POVs
  • Routines
  • Progress
  • Discipline

No links. No selling.

10% of posts → Direction

  • Pinned posts
  • Occasional ‘link in bio’ reminder
  • Stories

Rule

Desire first. Direction second.

Step 5

Use Stories to Point, Not Sell

Stories are your lowest-effort sales tool.

Story sequence (always the same):

  1. Context (question you get asked)
  2. Authority (this is what you follow)
  3. CTA (link in bio)

Frequency: 2–4 times per week is enough.

Stories warm people up so the website can close.

If You Do These, Sales Drop

Hard stops. Do not cross these lines.

  • DM selling
  • Over-explaining
  • Feature talk
  • Posting links with no context
  • Changing offers

Rule

Consistency beats creativity.

Your Weekly Operating System

Non-negotiable minimums.

  • ☑ Pinned post live
  • ☑ Normal posts continue
  • ☑ 2–4 story sequences
  • ☑ Reply to comments
  • ☑ Let the funnel work

If this feels boring, you’re doing it right.

You do not need to do more than this.

Post normally. Let the system do its job. Consistency beats intensity.
