1. Introduction

Raising The Bar Coaching ("we," "our," "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to improve user experience, analyze site performance, and deliver relevant content and advertisements.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit our website. They enable our website to recognize your device and remember your preferences.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website's basic functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze how users interact with our site.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable personalization features, such as remembering your preferences.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver tailored ads based on your browsing activity.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website.

For more information on how to manage cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services, such as analytics providers or advertisers, to place cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by the third party's privacy policies.
